Safety Amenities

In a world where safety and security are paramount, the SmartHouse 360 offers a suite of advanced safety products designed to protect your home and loved ones. Here's how our smart safety solutions can bring you peace of mind:

Smart Water Leak Sensors Leak Detection:
  • Get immediate alerts if a water leak is detected, helping prevent costly damage and mold growth.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Place sensors in key areas such as under sinks or near appliances to monitor for leaks around the clock.
  • Automatic Shutoff: Integrate with your smart home system to automatically shut off the water supply in case of a leak.
Smart Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detectors Instant Alerts:
  • Receive real-time notifications on your smartphone if smoke or carbon monoxide is detected.
  • Voice Alerts: Get clear, audible warnings within the home, ensuring everyone is alerted in case of an emergency.
  • Test and Maintenance Notifications: Stay informed when it's time to test or replace the batteries, ensuring your detectors are always functional.
Self-Protection Devices
  • A password system that will ensure no one who you don't know can't get in.
  • Shutters that will close when animals get close to ward them off.
